YOUR GOAL:

There are 7 levels. Each level will be a crucial question about the series. If answered correctly, Eleven is fed an Eggo waffle and grows stronger. If answered incorrectly, Eleven gets a nosebleed. At the end of the seven levels, If you have more Eggos than nosebleeds -- YOU WIN! Eleven and you will have the power to get Will back BUT if you give Eleven more nosebleeds than Eggos, Will is lost forever, and you have lost the game...

It is up to you to save these two young souls, GOOD LUCK!
